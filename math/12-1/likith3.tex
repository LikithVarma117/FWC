\section*{6.5 APPROXIMATIONS}

\noindent
Let $y=f(x)$. Let $\Delta x$ be a small increment in $x$.

\noindent
The increment in $y$ is $\Delta y=f(x+\Delta x)-f(x)$.

\noindent
The differential of $y$ is

\begin{align}
dy=\frac{dy}{dx}\Delta x
\end{align}
